Dog

Dogs are domestic descendants of wolf. Which are called domestic dogs, dericed from an extinct wolf known as Pleistocene wolves. Dogs were initially domesticated by hunter-getherers over 15 thousand years ago, and that was before the development of agriculture.

  • Family: Canidae
  • Scientific name: Canis lupus
  • Lifespan: 10 - 13 years
  • Daily sleep: 12 - 14 hours
  • Gestation period: 58 - 68 days
  • Higher classification: Wolf

Dogs are in different colors and sizes. Their sense includes vision, hearing, taste, and touch. Domestic dogs have two types of coat; "double" can be seen among dogs originating from cold areas and it is made up of a coarse guard hair and a soft down hair, or "single", with the topcoat only. Depending on the bread the may have occasional blaze, stripe or star of white fur on the chest or underside. According to wikipedia.
